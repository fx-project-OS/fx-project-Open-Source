Feedback slider

The "feedback slider" is located on the right side of the screen and can be opened



or closed.




The "feedback slider" gives information about the success of the last action or shows warnings and error messages that occurred, e.g. if a required field was not specified or similar. In case of a warning or error, the slider is automatically expanded after clicking on "Save" or similar - in case of success, however, it is collapsed.

The status of the confirmations is displayed with the usual traffic light colors, i.e.

Red =A fatal error occurred - the data was not saved.
Yellow =A warning has occurred and should be heeded. Depending on the severity of the warning, the data may or may not have been saved anyway. If the data was saved, an additional "green" ampelicon will also appear.
Green =All OK, the data was saved successfully.

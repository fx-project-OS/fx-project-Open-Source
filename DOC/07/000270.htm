Creation of meal and accommodation allowances at home and abroad


In this program function you can store the currently valid statutory per diems for additional expenses for meals and overnight expenses for domestic and international trips. For each country, you can specify per diems for a length of stay of less than 14 hours but at least 8 hours, less than 24 hours but at least 14 hours, and for 24 hours (in accordance with German tax law until 12/31/2013). Alternatively, you can declare per diems for each country for the day of arrival and departure as well as for 24 hours if the duration of stay is less than 24 hours but at least 8 hours (in accordance with German tax law as of 01.01.2014). The corresponding overnight flat rate can also be entered here.

With the help of the validity period you can determine the validity period of the respective per diems.

With the integrated search function, desired per diems can be found quickly and adjusted if necessary.

NOTE:
Make sure to always store the currently valid statutory per diems, otherwise trips can no longer be accounted for correctly for tax purposes!
This program function is a standard administrator program and can only be adapted by persons with corresponding administrator rights.

Accom.:

Here you can enter the flat rate for accommodation costs per night.

Overnight stay allowances can only be granted if the traveller is an employee. The employer can reimburse his employee for overnight expenses tax-free: In the amount of the expenses proven by receipt or, without proof, in the amount of the flat-rate amount, which varies from country to country, insofar as the employee did not receive the accommodation free of charge or at a reduced price from the employer or from a third party due to his employment relationship.

The tax-free payment of the lump sum is also not permitted in the case of overnight accommodation in a vehicle. In the case of use of a sleeping car or a ship's cabin, the lump sums may only be paid tax-free if the overnight stay has begun or ended in another accommodation.

For such days, the overnight allowances shall be deactivated.

Since 2008, entrepreneurs can no longer claim any lump sums for overnight stays at all. Costs for overnight stays can now only be claimed by entrepreneurs as business expenses in the amount actually incurred, which must be proven by receipt.
Always make sure to deposit the currently valid statutory flat rates, otherwise trips can no longer be correctly accounted for tax purposes!

Administration > Departments > Department Report

Listing the department structure with department managers and assigned employees


The department report lists all departments, their department heads and the persons assigned to them.




Determine selection criteria

Selection of the report and the highlighting criteria

In Determine selection criteria the button Person in Department is used to request the desired data.



Field Descriptions

Department:

In Department all departments are displayed.

Person:

In Person all Int. and Ext. employees are displayed.


Accept

Clicking this button saves the entered data and continues the program execution.




Department list

Persons and their departments

In Department list persons and their departments are contrasted.

In Determine selection criteria persons can be selected and thus highlighted.

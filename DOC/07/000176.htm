Working environment

After logging in, you will see the working environment in which you move around in fx-project.


The working environment of fx-project is divided into four main areas, which are described in more detail in the following pages:


Toolbar (at the top of the screen)

In addition to the fx-project logo and a search function, the toolbar contains the most important control options for fx-project, such as Control Center, Filters, Settings, Help, Logout, etc.


Menu bar (top, below the toolbar)

The individual program functions of fx-project are accessed via the functionally grouped menu items of the menu bar.


Workspace (centered, below the menu bar)

In the workspace of fx-project, the called program functions are processed incl. Selection of actions, input of data, display of reports or charts etc.


Footer (at the bottom of the screen)

The footer of fx-project contains user, date and time information as well as selection options of designs, languages and/or person units.

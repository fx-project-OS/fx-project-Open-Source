Daily allowances (= daily flat rates and non-monetary compensation) for one person's tarvels


Detailed daily travel expenses report

All possible information on the per diems (= per diems and per diems in kind) of a person's trips is listed here.




Selection criteria

Define selection criteria for the travel expenses per diem report.

A person for whom the report is to be created must always be selected.

Possible other optional criteria are the travel period, the project or the client.

NOTE:
The project filter works only here for the project selection. If no project is selected, all trips will be listed regardless of the set project filter.



Field Descriptions

Start Date:

If a date is written into this field, then all determined dates are limited to dates whose start date is after the date entered here.

Person:

This selection field is used to select the person whose data is to be edited or displayed depending on the action.

End Date:

If a date is written into this field, then all determined dates are limited to dates whose end date is before the date entered here.

Project:

The Project field lists the tasks (projects or tasks) for which you have the appropriate rights and the corresponding filter criteria apply.

Customer:

Here you can select the client of the activity (project, subproject or task).


Display

By clicking on this button, the list is generated and displayed. Depending on the defined selection criteria, the list is filtered accordingly.




Travel Expenses Daily Detail Report

Travel Expenses Daily Detail Report

All existing information on the daily data is listed clearly.

The report is divided into the individual trips sorted by start date.

The report is divided into three main areas:

  • The left day section contains for each day the indication of the weekday, the date, the period as well as the number of hours.
    • .
  • The middle per diem section contains, for each day, the information for meals and lodging, as well as the resulting total per day.

  • The right remuneration in kind section contains for each day the information for meals arranged by the employer, which are to be deducted from the per diems. The meals are divided into breakfast, lunch and dinner. The resulting total amount to be deducted per day is also listed.



Field Descriptions

Day:

Full day of the week name.

Date:

Used to enter or display a date of the day. If a date entry is required, the default value (today's date) is usually already preset.

Time Per.:

Displays the runtime of the project in a combined field.

Hours:

Number of hours according to context, e.g. number of overtime hours to be paid, etc.

Board:

Daily allowance for meals. This is dependent on the duration of travel for that day.

Accom.:

Here you can enter the flat rate for accommodation costs per night.

Overnight stay allowances can only be granted if the traveller is an employee. The employer can reimburse his employee for overnight expenses tax-free: In the amount of the expenses proven by receipt or, without proof, in the amount of the flat-rate amount, which varies from country to country, insofar as the employee did not receive the accommodation free of charge or at a reduced price from the employer or from a third party due to his employment relationship.

The tax-free payment of the lump sum is also not permitted in the case of overnight accommodation in a vehicle. In the case of use of a sleeping car or a ship's cabin, the lump sums may only be paid tax-free if the overnight stay has begun or ended in another accommodation.

For such days, the overnight allowances shall be deactivated.

Since 2008, entrepreneurs can no longer claim any lump sums for overnight stays at all. Costs for overnight stays can now only be claimed by entrepreneurs as business expenses in the amount actually incurred, which must be proven by receipt.
Always make sure to deposit the currently valid statutory flat rates, otherwise trips can no longer be correctly accounted for tax purposes!

Sum:

Here you specify whether the employee's hours per day are to be totalled or listed individually.

Breakf.:

Less meal arranged by the employer, in this case breakfast.

Lunch:

Less meal arranged by the employer, in this case lunch.

Supper:

Less meal arranged by the employer, in this case dinner.

Sum:

The actual total is displayed here.

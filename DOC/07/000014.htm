Control > General > Control Center

Overview of the most important tasks, events or appointments


The control center displays the most important data for daily work. These are:

  • Current messages of the day as a ticker display.
  • Graphical Project overview with a listing of tasks due in the next 4 weeks, sorted by urgency.
  • Information block about progress estimates still to be submitted with a direct link to the corresponding program function.
  • A list of unread emails and documents of the last four weeks.
  • In the appointment block all current standard and absence appointments such as sick leave, vacation or training are displayed.
  • Special reports can also be created in the report generator and displayed here.

Manage (create, modify or delete) freely definable capability groups, i.e. higher-level categories of individual capabilities.

Field Descriptions

Only Cust. Data:

Activate or deactivate an entry by placing a tick in the "checkbox".

Group:

This field displays the individual employee skill groups, that is, the employee areas that they cover.

By clicking on this button, the changes are made. The text of the button depends on the current action mode.

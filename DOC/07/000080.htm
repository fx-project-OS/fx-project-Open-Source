Administration > Departments > Department Administration

Administration of the department structure


In fx-project, departments and department hierarchies with responsible department managers can be defined. Departments restrict the access rights of the administrator role for personal data when used, so that only assigned departments and the departments below them can be accessed.




Manage departments

Manage departments

Departments can be defined here. Nesting in depth is possible. Departments are advantageous when selecting groups of people, etc.



Field Descriptions

Only Cust. Data:

Activate or deactivate an entry by placing a tick in the "checkbox".

Department:

Shows the department level with higher levels. e.g.: '0' - level 0 => company, '2-3' - subdepartment number 3, department number 2 etc.

Name of Department:

The department name is displayed in the mask with department level.

Head of Department:

Displays the head of a department.

Superior Department:

Specifies the parent department.

Cost Center:

Unique assignment number to a cost center.


Save

By clicking on this button, the changes are made. The text of the button depends on the current action mode.

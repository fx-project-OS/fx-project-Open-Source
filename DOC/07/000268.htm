EK/VK - Definition

Entry or deletion of the EK/VKs for the previously selected category.

When planning the target effort or the person budgets in the projects, these EC/ACs are used as a calculation basis to support you.

A field can be left "empty". Then the content of a possibly superior object or category will be used for the calculation. If no entry is found, 0.00 is used.

If both the purchase and the sales field are left "empty", this is equivalent to checking the "Delete" checkbox.



Field Descriptions

Field Name:

Display of the (sub)project name and capabilities.

Type:

Hourly rate - standard, skill, skill group.

PP:

This is the monetary value, the amount of which shows the financial expenditure for the company per hour of use of a worker or a material (counterpart to VK=sales price).

SP:

This is the monetary value, the amount of which shows the financial return for the company per day of use of a worker or a material (counterpart to EK=purchase price).

Del.:

This field indicates whether the related field for this profile may be deleted.

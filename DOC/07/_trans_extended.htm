Material Definition
No: 78
  Path: Creation > Materials > Material Definition

Creation and management of immediate, consumable and/or time material definitions.

Immediate materials are materials that are assigned once to tasks, whereby the corresponding costs and/or revenues are transferred directly to the project budget, e.g. computers, printers, etc.

Consumable materials are materials that can be consumed by a person on a unit basis (piece, meter, etc.) on a daily basis, e.g. cables, sockets, etc.

Time materials are materials that can be consumed by a person on an hourly basis and to the day, analogous to time recording, e.g. company cars, rented rooms, etc.

Allowances: km/Non-monetary Renumeration
No: 269
  Path: Creation > Travel Allowances > Allowances: km/Non-monetary Renumeration

Settings for travel expense per diems, such as mileage allowance or meals.

The automatic calculations of the travel expense program are based on the travel expense flat rates stored here.

In this program function you can store the currently valid statutory mileage allowances for the various vehicle types and passengers, as well as per diems and reductions for meals for domestic and international trips.

This program function is a standard administrator program and can only be customized by individuals with appropriate administrator privileges.

NOTE:
Make sure to always store the currently valid statutory per diems, otherwise trips can no longer be accounted for correctly for tax purposes! These can be found in the official country list of the Federal Ministry of Finance.

Allowances: Overnight Stay
No: 270
  Path: Creation > Travel Allowances > Allowances: Overnight Stay

In this program function you can store the currently valid statutory per diems for additional expenses for meals and overnight expenses for domestic and international trips. For each country, you can specify per diems for a length of stay of less than 14 hours but at least 8 hours, less than 24 hours but at least 14 hours, and for 24 hours (in accordance with German tax law until 12/31/2013). Alternatively, you can declare per diems for each country for the day of arrival and departure as well as for 24 hours if the duration of stay is less than 24 hours but at least 8 hours (in accordance with German tax law as of 01.01.2014). The corresponding overnight flat rate can also be entered here.

With the help of the validity period you can determine the validity period of the respective per diems.

With the integrated search function, desired per diems can be found quickly and adjusted if necessary.

NOTE:
Make sure to always store the currently valid statutory per diems, otherwise trips can no longer be accounted for correctly for tax purposes!
This program function is a standard administrator program and can only be adapted by persons with corresponding administrator rights.

TR (Sum)
No: 34

Cost and performance accounting (CAC) is an institutionalized information system that contains all economically evaluable projects of information gathering and information processing about incurred or planned business transactions and is primarily directed at company employees.

Cost and performance accounting is used to determine cost and performance information for solving operational management tasks.

The goals and tasks of cost accounting are the profitability control of departments or companies, the calculation and evaluation of cost units, and thus also the procurement of information for product and pricing policy.
